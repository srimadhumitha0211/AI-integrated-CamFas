My Leave History

⏳ Dead Application — What does it mean?

  • If your CA does not respond within 3 days, the application is automatically marked as Dead.
  • Dead applications are stored in your history for reference (Excel sheet record is maintained).
  • You can re-apply or send an apology to the faculty using the Send Apology button.

🚨 Couldn't Apply Before Leave? (Emergency Situation)

  • If you were in an emergency and couldn't apply in advance, click 🚨 Emergency Leave after returning.
  • An apology message will be auto-attached to your request and sent to the faculty.
  • You can also write a custom apology from the Send Apology button on any past leave entry.
