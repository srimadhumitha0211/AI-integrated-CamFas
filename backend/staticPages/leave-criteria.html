Eligibility Criteria for taking leave

* A student is permitted to take a maximum of only 3 leave days per month.

* The student must maintain a minimum of 70% attendance in all subjects to be eligible to write the semester examinations.

* If the attendance percentage falls below 70%, the student will not be permitted to appear for the semester exams.

* Attendance will be calculated based on the total number of working days in the academic month.

* Medical leave will be considered only with valid medical documentation.

* Continuous absence without prior permission may lead to disciplinary action.

* Students are responsible for ensuring their attendance meets the required criteria before the examination.
