Eligibility Criteria for taking leave

A student is permitted to take a maximum of only 3 leave days per month.

The student must maintain a minimum of 70% attendance.

If attendance falls below 70%, the student cannot appear for exams.

Attendance is calculated based on total working days.

Medical leave requires valid documentation.

Continuous absence may lead to disciplinary action.

Students must ensure required attendance before exams.
